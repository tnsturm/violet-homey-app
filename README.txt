Bring your PoolDigital Violet or BADU Blue pool controller into Homey over the local network - no cloud account needed. See live water chemistry, temperatures and equipment status, and control the filter pump, lights and PV-surplus mode, all through Homey Insights and Flow.

An optional Langelier (LSI) water-balance safety net continuously computes whether your water is corrosive, scaling or balanced, and warns you before it damages pipes, tiles or the heater. Alarm and dosing status are available as Flow triggers too.
